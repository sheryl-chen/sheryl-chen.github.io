hi! i'm sheryl.

i'm a new york city-based professional who specializes in marketing, business analytics, and digital strategy.

i graduated from nyu stern with a bachelor's of science in marketing and computing & data science. i'm a self-taught coder, amateur graphic designer, aspiring polyglot, beauty/skincare afficionado, devoted potterhead, proud nerd, and loud proponent of the oxford comma.

i'm a born-and-bred new yorker (read: tough and ambitious). this city has taught me the value hard work and independence. it has also taught me to never stop hustling, whether that means taking interesting online courses, dabbling in new hobbies, or working on cool side projects (like this website! did i mention i coded and designed this entire site from scratch?). i firmly believe that every minute we spend not learning something is a minute wasted.

what am i currently up to?

i'm starting my post-graduate career as a category specialist in health at walmart ecommerce/jet.

i'm a co-director of an award-winning, competitive urban dance team called synchronic dance team.

i'm reading making a point: the persnickety story of english punctuation by david crystal and balik kampung by verena tay (fun fact: i picked up balik kampung on a trip to singapore!)

i'm planning a blog where i hope to share my experiences as an apa woman in new york and attempt to navigate my thoughts on today's complex issues.
